How PawCare started

2019 — A small rescue

Founded by a group of pet-lovers who rescued 3 injured strays and vowed to do more.

2020 — Community network

Local volunteers joined, small vet partners came on board and donations started pouring in.

2022 — Digital launch

We launched PawCare online to accept secure donations, show live ledgers, and help adopters connect.

Today

Expanding shelters, empowering volunteers & delivering healthcare to thousands of pets.

Our Aim

To create a sustainable, transparent network for rescue, treatment and adoption. We believe in openness — donors can view receipts and admins update spends in real time.
