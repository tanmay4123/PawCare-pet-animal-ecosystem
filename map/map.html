🐾 Our Story

PawCare started when a stray kitten named Mimi was adopted by us and accidently few months later jumped off from 4th floor Ravet, Pune. It took nearly an hour to find a vet open nearby — that delay changed how we saw animal care forever. From that day, PawCare’s mission became clear: make emergency help accessible to every animal in need.
