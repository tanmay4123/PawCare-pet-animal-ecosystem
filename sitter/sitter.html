❓ Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How are sitters verified on PawCare? +
Every sitter goes through a multi-step verification: government ID check, police verification, an in-person home visit by our team, and a pet first-aid quiz. Only those who pass all steps are listed.
What if my pet gets sick during the stay? +
All bookings include PawCare's emergency vet coverage up to ₹10,000. Your sitter is trained to recognise signs of illness and will contact our 24/7 support line immediately. We have partner vets in 30+ cities.
Can I visit the sitter's home before booking? +
Absolutely — and we strongly encourage it. Every sitter offers a free meet-and-greet so you and your pet can get comfortable with the environment before committing to a booking.
How do I cancel or change my booking? +
Cancel free of charge up to 48 hours before the start of your booking for a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours receive a 50% refund. Changes to dates are free if the sitter is available.
Are medications and special diets handled? +
Yes. When booking, note your pet's dietary needs and medication schedule. Most sitters are trained to handle medication administration. If your pet has complex needs, filter for sitters with "Med admin" in their amenities.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept UPI, all major credit and debit cards, net banking, and wallets. Payment is held securely and only released to the sitter after your pet's stay begins.
